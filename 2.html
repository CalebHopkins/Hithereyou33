Tetris

Tetris- a classic and a worldwide sensation- has been adapted into this flash game. Featuring new and fresh graphics as well as lovable sound effects, Tetris is the perfect puzzle solving game. In Tetris, objects fall from the sky and it is up to the player to position these. When a full line of blocks are in place, they will vanish and give the player score, but if they stack up and reach the top of the screen, then it is game over.
